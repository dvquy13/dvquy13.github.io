5 years ago, I first learned web development through Miguel Grinberg's Flask Mega-Tutorial series (https://lnkd.in/gy6WCFeq). That experience was transformative—seeing how someone could break down complex topics into digestible, hands-on chapters that actually work. I always dreamed of creating something similar in my own domain.

Fast forward to March 2025 when the team at Full Stack Data Science invited me to give a webinar on recommender systems. That invitation became the catalyst - I crafted an outline, built a complete demo, and dove deep into session-based RecSys implementation.

It felt only natural to turn this into a comprehensive blog series. After all, I'd already invested the effort in building the demo and implementation - why not 
share the complete journey?

After way too much procrastination, here are my 7 chapters of "Implementing a Recommender System": https://lnkd.in/gVtTUNVE

It’s for anyone looking for an end-to-end tutorial on building a real-time RecSys with session-based modeling, model server and API service, vector databases, and a working demo.

And just like feedback is central to ML and RecSys, it's central to my work too. I'd absolutely love your comments, suggestions, and thoughts as you dive in.

Thanks to everyone who's been part of this journey. Hope you enjoy the series! 🙏
